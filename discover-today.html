Question-2: What is the difference between innerHTML, innerText, and textContent?

innerHTML:
The HTML content (including tags and text) within an element.
innerText:
The rendered visible text content of an element.
textContent:
The raw text content of an element, including all text within its descendant nodes (even hidden elements, script tags, and style tags).
